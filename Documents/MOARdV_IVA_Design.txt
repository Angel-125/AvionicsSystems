Notes on the Summer 2017 MOARdV Flight Systems IVA design patterns.

Toggle switches, modular push buttons: size = 0.4
When switches / buttons are parented to another switch / button, relative spacing is:
 bordered button: lateral spacing 0.06
 non-bordered (Action Groups, etc): 0.045
 bordered, no labels: 0.055

Signal lamp: 0.5

FDAI: size = 1

X-Pointer = 0.72
